关于我About Me

目前是同济大学交通信息与控制工程专业的在读博士研究生。研究工作致力于将数据驱动的机器学习技术与经典运筹学算法相结合，解决智慧城市和高体量出行平台中的核心决策痛点。


近两年来，与滴滴出行算法团队开展了深度项目与学术合作，主导设计了多项端到端预测-决策耦合框架。相关算法已在真实城市级仿真环境及上海临港新片区需求响应式公交服务中上线运行，实现了学术理论在工业界的实体落地。

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Transportation Information and Control Engineering at Tongji University. My research interests lie at the intersection of data-driven machine learning, operations research, and intelligent transportation systems.


Recently, I have been working closely with industry leader Didi Chuxing on end-to-end predict-then-optimize frameworks, decision-focused learning under uncertainty, and large-scale mobility optimization for ride-hailing and demand-responsive transit systems.

研究方向Research Interests

Predict-then-Optimize

端到端预测决策耦合。针对决策不确定性、仿真损失不可微及黑盒模型设计损失函数与梯度估计方法。

Decision-focused learning algorithms designed for end-to-end combinatorial and nonlinear optimization under uncertainty.

Large-scale Optimization

大规模随机优化与启发式求解。利用增广拉格朗日（ADMM）和问题解耦技术提升大规模VRP求解效率。

Stochastic optimization, ADMM decomposition, and heuristic approaches for massive transport networks.

需求响应式出行系统

Demand Responsive Mobility

多模式供给协同优化。涵盖网约车供需双边不确定性下的前瞻性车辆调度，以及灵活公交在长期定价与实时路径（VRP）上的多阶段随机优化。

Co-optimization of multi-modal transit. Integrating predictive fleet rebalancing under dual-sided uncertainty with joint pricing and routing for flexible on-demand bus services.

工业级算法落地

Industrial Deployment

学术成果工程化应用。主导的调度与路径规划算法在滴滴出行城市级时空仿真环境进行深度验证，并成功上线于上海临港新片区智慧公交系统。

Translating research into practice. Algorithms verified in city-scale simulators at Didi Chuxing and successfully deployed in real-world transit systems in Shanghai Lin-gang Area.
