About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Transportation Information and Control Engineering at Tongji University. My research interests lie at the intersection of data-driven machine learning, operations research, and intelligent transportation systems.


Recently, I have been working closely with industry leader Didi Chuxing on end-to-end predict-then-optimize frameworks, decision-focused learning under uncertainty, and large-scale mobility optimization for ride-hailing and demand-responsive transit systems.

Research Interests

Predict-then-Optimize

Decision-focused learning algorithms designed for end-to-end combinatorial and nonlinear optimization under uncertainty.

Large-scale Optimization

Stochastic optimization, ADMM decomposition, and heuristic approaches for massive transport networks.

Vehicle Rebalancing

Predictive and anticipatory fleet rebalancing strategies for massive ride-hailing networks considering two-sided uncertainties.

Demand Responsive Transit

Joint pricing, vehicle routing, and strategic scheduling for flexible customized bus systems.
